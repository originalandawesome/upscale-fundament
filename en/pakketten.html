Additional — Payroll & HR

Staff, or on the payroll yourself?

Payroll administration

Do you employ staff, or are you on your own BV's payroll? Payroll administration runs through our regular payroll partner and is charged separately. As an indication:

  • € 25 per month for the subscription (the overarching work per company)
  • € 11.50 per month per employee
  • When taking over an existing, extensive payroll administration, one-off setup costs may be charged

2026 price level, indicative; no rights can be derived from these amounts. All amounts excluding VAT. Feel free to ask us for current prices for your situation.

Special deal for director-shareholders (DGA)

If you're the only one on your BV's payroll, the regular route works out at € 36.50 per month. That's why we've arranged a special deal for DGAs: an all-in rate of € 330 per year, payable after you receive your first payslip. That works out at € 27.50 per month.

The only condition is that your salary stays the same for the rest of the year. If you do want to change it mid-year, that change is charged at € 95 per hour. In our experience, even with one such change the deal is still at least as favourable as the regular route. And choosing is entirely voluntary: the regular method is always available.

HR advice

Do you have staff, but aren't big enough yet for your own HR employee? Through our payroll partner you can also purchase HR advice (€ 95 per hour).

And if you do have someone on HR, an experienced sparring partner is sometimes exactly what's needed.
