Sheet 02 — Services

You do what you're good at. We handle the rest.

We handle the complete administrative and tax process for Dutch SMEs: from sole traders and partnerships to BVs and foundations. There is a reason you do what you do and didn't become a tax advisor. And that's exactly how it should be: you do what you're good at, we handle the rest.

S·01

Bookkeeping

Your complete administration in SnelStart. Sales invoices, purchase invoices, receipts and bank transactions: everything in one place, always up to date. You don't have to pick accounting software yourself; we set everything up together during the intake.

How we do it →
S·02

Annual accounts

At the end of the year we close your books and prepare the annual accounts. Correct and within the legal framework. The basis for your tax returns — and for a mortgage application, for example.

How we do it →
S·03

Tax returns

All business tax returns are taken care of: VAT (btw), income tax, corporate income tax. Always following the same principle: you receive the draft first, and we only file after your approval. So you stay in control of what goes to the Dutch tax authorities.

How we do it →
S·04

Payroll taxes and payroll administration

On your own BV's payroll, or employing staff? We take care of the complete payroll administration: from applying for your payroll tax number to the monthly payslips, the payroll tax returns and ready-made payment files for your bank.

Payroll administration is charged separately, on top of your fixed package.

See the rates →
S·05

Tax and administrative advice

Questions about investing, your provisional assessment, converting your sole proprietorship into a BV, or winding down your business? As tax advisors we think along with you all year round, not just at filing time.

Thinking along and quick questions are simply part of the deal. Does your question become substantial and require real research? Then we charge it as additional work — always flagged in advance, and honestly: you usually sense perfectly well when a question is heading that way.

Included as standard

Tax optimisation: included as standard

The foundation of our work is accurate bookkeeping and correct, on-time tax returns. That is what you pay us for. But because we are tax advisors, we apply tax optimisation on top wherever possible: we look at current Dutch tax law and apply it as favourably as possible for you.

That is what sets us apart from a regular bookkeeping firm: processing what you send in is our starting point, not the end point.
