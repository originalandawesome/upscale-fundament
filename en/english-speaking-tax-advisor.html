What we do

We run your complete bookkeeping in SnelStart and take care of your annual accounts and all your Dutch tax returns: VAT (btw), income tax, corporate income tax and payroll taxes. And because we are tax advisors, we apply Dutch tax law as favourably as possible for you. That optimisation comes standard.
