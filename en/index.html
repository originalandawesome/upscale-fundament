01 — What we do

Your complete books and taxes, off your plate.

We run your complete bookkeeping in SnelStart and take care of your annual accounts and all your Dutch tax returns: VAT (btw), income tax, corporate income tax and payroll taxes.

The difference with a regular bookkeeping firm? We don't just process what you send in. As tax advisors, we apply Dutch tax law as favourably as possible for you. That optimisation comes standard.

02 — Who we work with

From your first invoice to a business with staff.

English-speaking entrepreneurs across the Netherlands: freelancers and sole traders (eenmanszaak), partnerships (VOF), private limited companies (BV) and foundations. Whether you have just registered with the Chamber of Commerce or have been running your business for years.

We work fully digitally and by phone, in English. It works exactly the same wherever you are in the Netherlands. And if you would rather meet in person first? That is always possible, by appointment.

03 — Why Upscale

We have never had to advertise.

Our clients come by word of mouth and they stay — unless they go back into employment or emigrate. That says enough: we are not after quick wins, but build long-term relationships by delivering good work with a personal touch.

You always receive a draft of every tax return first, and we only file after your explicit approval. Nothing goes to the Dutch tax authorities without you knowing exactly what is in it.
