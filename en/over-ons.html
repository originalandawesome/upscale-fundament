Sheet 06 — About us

Two tax advisors, one principle: good work sells itself.

Who we are

Vincent started out over twenty years ago as an employed tax advisor and, after a few years, founded his own practice: Upscale Consultancy. His first client? A friend of his mother's. He never advertised, and yet the client base grew: new clients come in by word of mouth, and clients don't leave. Except when they go back into employment or emigrate. The best proof: that friend of his mother's is still a client today.

At some point the number of clients grew so fast that Vincent could no longer handle it alone. Over the years he had worked very pleasantly with Raj, so he asked whether Raj felt like starting a company together. And so it happened: Upscale Consultancy became Upscale Consultancy Financial Services B.V. Raj, like Vincent, started out as a tax advisor over twenty years ago and wanted to grow bigger by running his own business. A perfect match.

Meanwhile we're so busy again that our team has been expanded once more. That we've never had to advertise is something we're secretly proud of: it reflects who we are and how our clients experience us. It's also the atmosphere within our company. We stand for quality. We don't chase short-term profit, but build excellent client relationships by delivering fantastic work with a personal touch.
